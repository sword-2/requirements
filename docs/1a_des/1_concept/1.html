Goals

Table 1 attempts to capture some simple goals. The modules were assumed to be most significant. If all diligence was used to communicate with modules, it is possible the SWORD project documents currently on the crosswire site and wiki could just become one category in a module "library". As cool as the SWORD project is, it was perceived to be one form of many forms of evangelism where evangelism could be a library category. For this reason, a second requirements document is being considered for the SWORD project as a missionary outreach. Goals were divided into existing and consideration. Some existing ones were copied from the current crosswire internet site(s) or observed in existing front ends. Goals for consideration were added either due to not understanding the program, or as a check to ensure something wasn't inadvertently skipped.
